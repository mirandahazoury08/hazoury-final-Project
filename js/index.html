Your State's Laws on Abortion

The abortion landscape in the United States is fragmented and increasingly polarized. Many states have implemented restrictions or bans that make it challenging, if not impossible, for people to access abortion care. Conversely, other states have taken steps to protect abortion rights and ensure access. This interactive map categorizes states into one of five groups based on their current abortion policies. Users can select any state to view detailed information about its abortion policies, characteristics of its residents, and key abortion statistics.

Click on the different States to learn where they land on restrictive laws of abortion.

The five different groups are defined as: Very Restrictive States, Restrictive States, States with Some Restriction, Protective States, and very Protective State.
